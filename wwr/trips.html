Trip Options

Family Float Adventure

Difficulty: Easy · Duration: Half-day · Age: 6+

A calm and scenic section of the river, perfect for beginners and families with young children. Gentle rapids, plenty of time to relax, and an easy introduction to rafting.

Classic Canyon Run

Difficulty: Moderate · Duration: Full-day · Age: 12+

Our most popular trip. A full-day adventure with a mix of calm stretches, fun rapids, and beautiful canyon views. Includes a riverside lunch and photo opportunities.

Extreme Rapids Challenge

Difficulty: Advanced · Duration: Full-day · Age: 16+

Designed for thrill-seekers. Powerful rapids, technical paddling, and non-stop action with our most experienced guides. Strong swimming ability required.
